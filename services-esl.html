Workplace English Teaching and Training / ESL

Most people want to be good at what they do. They want to excel, and they want to take on new and interesting challenges at work.

Unfortunately, Canadian immigrants are often held back by poor English skills.

The good news is that training them in English has tremendous benefits for both employers and employees.

The Benefits of on-site English Training:

  • Confidence begets Confidence. A company’s faith in its employees is rewarded with better morale and increased teamwork, as well as reduced turnover. Better communication also means fewer interpersonal problems.
  • Save Money. Better communication and understanding leads to reduced error rates and less waste. This also means a safer work environment. People trained in English are also more likely to read internal documents, such as manuals, newsletters, and safety documentation.
  • Identify and Empower those with Potential. English training is a great way to develop (and identify!) those with the character and skillset to go further within your company. People with real leadership potential may not have the communication skills to unlock it. Essentially this equates to an expanded hiring pool of people within the company – people who are already trained in its processes and culture.
